Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Plastic Detox (plasticdetox.org) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it.

What We Collect

Analytics data. We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use our site. This collects anonymized data including pages visited, time on site, device type, and approximate location (country/city level). We do not collect or store personally identifiable information through analytics.

Email address. If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address. This is stored and processed by Brevo (formerly Sendinblue), our email service provider. We use your email only to send periodic tips about reducing plastic exposure. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in every email.

Quiz results. Your quiz results are stored locally in your browser (localStorage) so you can return to them later. This data never leaves your device and is not sent to any server.

What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect names, phone numbers, or payment information.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • We do not use tracking pixels or retargeting ads.

Cookies

Google Analytics uses cookies to distinguish unique visitors and sessions. These are first party cookies set by Google's analytics script. No advertising cookies are used on this site.

Affiliate Links

Our store contains affiliate links to Amazon and other retailers. When you click these links and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. These links are clearly marked. Affiliate partners may use their own cookies to track referrals. We have no control over third party cookie policies.

Third Party Services

  • Google Analytics (GA4) for anonymous site usage data. Google's Privacy Policy
  • Brevo for email newsletter delivery. Brevo's Privacy Policy
  • GitHub Pages for website hosting. GitHub's Privacy Statement

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time.
  • Clear your quiz data by clicking "Start Over" in the quiz or clearing your browser's local storage.
  • Opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt Out Browser Add On.
  • Request deletion of your email from our mailing list by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

This site is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please email us at hello@plasticdetox.org.

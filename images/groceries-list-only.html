Grocery Guide

Grocery Plastic Exposure:
The 3 Zone Cheat Sheet

🔴 Always buy plastic free
1
Meat & SeafoodHigh fat + long contact = maximum leaching
  • Buy from the butcher counter in paper wrapping
  • Transfer to glass immediately if pre-packaged
2
Cheese & Cooking OilsFat absorbs BPA and phthalates from packaging
  • Buy cheese from deli counter in wax paper
  • Buy oils in glass bottles only
3
Canned Tomatoes & Hot FoodAcidity + heat = extreme BPA leaching
  • Buy tomato products in glass jars
  • Transfer rotisserie chicken from plastic immediately
🟡 Buy in plastic, transfer at home
4
Milk, Yogurt, Berries, Leafy GreensPour into glass pitchers and bowls when you get home
🟢 Safe in plastic
5
Frozen Vegs, Dry Pasta, Bananas, NutsDry, frozen, or thick peel = negligible exposure
Focus on the red zone. Skip the guilt on the green zone. plasticdetox.org

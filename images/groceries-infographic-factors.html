Grocery Guide

4 Factors That Make Plastic
Packaging Dangerous

🧈
Fat Content
BPA and phthalates are fat soluble. Fatty foods absorb more chemicals from plastic packaging than lean foods.
Biggest factor
🍋
Acidity
Acidic foods (tomatoes, citrus, vinegar) break down plastic faster and accelerate chemical leaching.
Major factor
⏱️
Contact Time
The longer food sits in plastic, the more it absorbs. Deli meat on a tray for 5 days vs. a banana in a bag for 10 minutes.
Moderate factor
🌡️
Temperature
Heat massively accelerates leaching. Frozen = safe. Room temp = moderate. Hot food in plastic = maximum risk.
Multiplier
When all 4 factors line up (fatty + acidic + long time + warm), the exposure is extreme. When none apply, it is negligible.
Not all plastic contact with food is equally harmful. plasticdetox.org

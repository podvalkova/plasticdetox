Grocery Guide

Plastic in Groceries:
What Really Matters

🔴
Buy Plastic Free
Fatty, acidic, or long contact
  • Meat & deli
  • Cheese
  • Cooking oils
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Baby food
  • Hot prepared food
🟡
Transfer at Home
OK to buy but re-store in glass
  • Milk & yogurt
  • Butter
  • Berries & soft fruit
  • Leafy greens
  • Condiments
  • Sliced bread
🟢
Safe in Plastic
Dry, frozen, or thick peel
  • Bananas & avocados
  • Frozen vegetables
  • Dry pasta & rice
  • Cereal & granola
  • Nuts & seeds
  • Onions & potatoes
Focus on the red zone. The green zone is not worth worrying about. plasticdetox.org

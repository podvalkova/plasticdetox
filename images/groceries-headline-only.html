Grocery Guide

Plastic in Groceries:
What Really Matters

🔴
Buy Plastic Free
Fatty, acidic, or long contact
  • Bottled water
  • Tea in plastic bags
  • Baby food & formula
  • Meat & seafood
  • Cooking oils
  • Nut butters
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Hot prepared food
🟡
Transfer at Home
OK to buy but re-store in glass
  • Chocolate
  • Cheese
  • Milk & sour cream
  • Yogurt
  • Butter
  • Berries & soft fruit
  • Condiments
  • Protein powder
🟢
Safe in Plastic
Dry, frozen, or thick peel
  • Bananas & avocados
  • Oranges & melons
  • Frozen vegetables
  • Frozen fruit
  • Dry pasta & rice
  • Coffee beans
  • Spices & dried herbs
  • Nuts & seeds
Focus on the red zone. The green zone is not worth worrying about. plasticdetox.org
